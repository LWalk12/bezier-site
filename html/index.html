A Fast, Free, Reliable Attendance Tracker

Hosting and Joining sessions

Bezier allows you to host and join sessions using QR codes and links. Creating a session will generate a one-time QR code. Once scanned for the first time, participants will be put into your session, allowing you to track their attendance for every future meeting! Hosts have the ability to customize their sessions to the fullest; give it a special name, choose who belongs and who doesn’t, and track your participants for free, forever. Joining a session is as easy as tying your shoe. Scan the QR code with your app, and you are officially in attendance. Participants have the ability to leave sessions at any time, and are able to join off of links created by the host as well.

For free, forever.

Bezier is completely free. Create and join as many sessions as you see fit, and you will never be charged a dime. Bezier exists to make your attendance tracking needs to the next level.

Any problems? Contact us.

The Bezier team is constantly working hard to ensure the app is running smoothly. Whether you have questions or concerns, reach out with us via email to start a new conversation!
